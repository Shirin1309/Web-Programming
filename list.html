TRIP TO MUNNAR

EXPLORING THE NATURE

The places we plans to go was listed below

  1. Eravikulam National Park
    • Best time:Sep to may
    • Entry fee:INR 65/Indian Visitor
    • Timing:08:00AM – 04:30PM
    Eravikulam National Park
    Eravikulam National Park is the home to endangered NilgiriTahrs. Declared as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  2. Mattupetty Dam
    • Best time:August to May
    • Entry fee:INR 10/person (Speed boating Fees: INR 500/5 persons)
    • Timing:09:30AM – 05:00PM
    Mattupetty Dam
    One of the major tourist attractions in Munnar, this water reservoir in Idukki district is located around 13km from the heart of the hill station.
